Bringing Texas BBQ to the Lou

Who are We?

Grinning Bull BBQ is a mobile food vendor and full-scale catering service, bringing world class meat smoked with a Texas-style flare to events in the greater St. Louis area.

"A taste of Texas in every slice."

Owner and pitmaster Kevin Bulgerin grew up in Texas, and developed a passion for great barbeque through years of working in the restaurant business. After he and his growing family moved to St. Louis, Kevin made it a goal to bring his Texas roots to the barbeque scene, and Grinning Bull BBQ was born in 2022.
